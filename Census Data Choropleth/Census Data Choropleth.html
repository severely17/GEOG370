This map shows the poverty rate of each county in New Jersey in 2019, showing the total poverty rate, not separated by age. The highest poverty rate is 13.8% in Cumberland County and the lowest being 3.7% in Morris County. The average poverty rate in 2019 for New Jersey was 9.2%, continuing the decline since 2013. This 9.2% poverty rate is signifcantly lower than the national average, which was 10.5% in 2019.
